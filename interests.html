music

I make electronic and noise music (ok, music is a stretch) with Ableton and Ultrabox (though most of my stuff was made with Jummbox). I've dabbled a bit with FL Studio and Reaper, I've tried famiTracker too (sorry tracker purists), but right now, my main focus is getting used to Ableton. I use Surge synthesizer primarily. I also use Ableton to record guitar demos. I don't have any finished songs yet, but hopefully that'll change soon.

15/6/24: Some songs I've played/are working on are No Remorse by Metallica and Ex-Creations by Fall of Troy (going to try and record these some time). I mostly play indie/alt rock and metal adjacent stuff but I also dabble in a bit of jazz.

Guitar: Ibanez gio

Amp: crush 35RT

I started on a cheap yamaha classical guitar.

I've played piano since I was too young to remember. I'm also a percussionist (derogatory).
